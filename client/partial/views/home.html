VBOX Captures Conditions...

02/11/2020

Our VBOX in-car camera system was capturing the action last weekend on the Cotswold Stages Rally at Down Ampney.

The weather at the event can only be described as atrocious. Crews battled it out over 7 slippery stages, in which many would suffer due to the conditions. Our crew was no different, severely hamstrung by a misfire from Stage 2 up until the last two Stages, despite the best efforts from their service team. However, they did fix the problem for the final two Stages, where they set top 5 times and climbed to 14th Overall and 4th in Class. Which is a good result considering the severe conditions and the technical issues they had during the day!
